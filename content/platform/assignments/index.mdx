# Assignments

Assignments let teachers create assessable tasks within courses. Students submit their work, and teachers can review and grade submissions individually or in bulk.

## How Assignments Work

Each assignment is linked to a course activity and can contain one or more tasks. Students complete tasks, upload files, or write code, and then submit their work for grading.

<Callout>
Assignments must be enabled in your [Organization Settings](/platform/organizations/settings) to be available.
</Callout>

## Key Features

- **Multi-task assignments** — Break assignments into individual tasks, each with its own submission and grade.
- **File uploads** — Students can attach files as part of their submission.
- **Submission tracking** — Teachers see submission status for each student and task.
- **Grading** — Grade individual tasks or entire assignments. Scores are recorded in the student's [Trail](/platform/courses/trails).
